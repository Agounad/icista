Submission Guidelines

ICISTA 2027 invites original, high-quality research contributions in all areas of intelligent systems and emerging technologies. All submissions will be peer-reviewed according to IEEE standards.

Full Papers

Manuscripts must be prepared in IEEE two-column format and should be 4–6 pages in length, including references, figures, and tables. Submissions must be written in English and comply with IEEE formatting guidelines.

All submissions must be original, unpublished, and not under review elsewhere. Each paper will be evaluated based on originality, technical quality, relevance, and clarity.

Abstracts

Authors who wish to present without publication may submit an abstract for evaluation. Accepted abstracts will be included in the conference program and scheduled for presentation.

Submission Guidelines

  • All submissions must be written in English
  • Papers must be original and not submitted elsewhere
  • Only PDF submissions are accepted
  • Minimum length: 4 pages (IEEE format)
  • All figures, tables, and references included in page limit
  • Submission must be made via chairconf system

Paper Presentation

Accepted papers will be scheduled for oral presentation based on reviewer evaluation and technical relevance.
