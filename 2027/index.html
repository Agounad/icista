About ICISTA 2027

The International Conference on Intelligent Systems, Technologies and Applications (ICISTA 2027) provides an international forum for researchers, academics, engineers, and industry professionals to present and discuss recent advances in Artificial Intelligence, intelligent systems, signal and image processing, electronics, robotics, as well as emerging and sustainable technologies.


The conference aims to foster collaboration among academia, research institutions, and industry, with the goal of developing innovative intelligent solutions to address current and future technological challenges.
