International Conference Organizational Structure

The ICISTA 2027 committee structure brings together distinguished experts from academia, research institutions, and industry to ensure a high-quality and inclusive technical program. The committees encompass leadership, strategic guidance, technical oversight, and community engagement, supporting the successful organization and scientific excellence of the conference.
